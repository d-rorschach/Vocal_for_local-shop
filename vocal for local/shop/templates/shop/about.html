Vocal For Local Shop!

Welcome to our "Vocal For Local" Shop. It is an online E-commerce website built by Prayas Pal and Aparajita Paul, to help the local business or shop owners, who have faced some major losses during the COVID-19 pandemic and lockdown. This site gives them a platform to showcase all the product they sell. For buyers our site is the best place to get all the Groceries, Medicines, Cloths and Handcrafts within an hour, without the hassle of standing in a queue outside the shops and also avoid the crowd. #Stay_home_stay_Safe! Also all the products will be from a local shop so in case of any difficulty replacement can also be done really quick.
